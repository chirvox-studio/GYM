The Beginning

FROM PASSION
TO PURPOSE

GYM Fitness was founded in 2019 by Vikram Shetty, a competitive powerlifter who saw a gap in 's fitness landscape: no gym that combined serious training infrastructure with genuinely expert coaching.

Starting with 800 sq ft and three trainers,GYM now spans 5,000 sq ft across two floors, hosts over 500 active members, and runs 8 daily training batches across disciplines.

The mission hasn't changed: give every person who walks through that door the tools and the guidance to become the best version of themselves — regardless of where they start.

⚡ New member inquiries tripled within the first month of our new website launch.
